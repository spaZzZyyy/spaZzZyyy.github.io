Player Origins

Soland "spZzZyyy" Chen is a fps competetor currently competing in Riot Game's title Valorant.
Origionating in Valve title Counter-Strike Global Offensive, Soland transitioned to being a player in Valorant
back when Valorant beta was first released in April 7, 2020 The release date of beta aligned
with Soland's entrance to University and tried out and made it on to the University of Colorado Boulder's Valorant team
There he competed in colleigate play showing a highly aggressive style in a role of entry fragging.
After playing on multiple iterations of the University roster, the third year into University Soland moved on
from his position on the team to play in tier 2 scene.
